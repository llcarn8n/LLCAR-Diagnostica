## 1. Li Auto

- **Language:** en

Li Auto Inc. is a Chinese electric vehicle manufacturer headquartered in Beijing, with manufacturing facilities in Changzhou. Founded by Li Xiang in 2015, the company is best known for producing electric vehicles equipped with range extender petrol engines.

## 2. Li L7

- **URL:** https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Li_L7
- **Language:** en
- **Length:** 301 chars

The Li L7 is a range-extender luxury mid-size crossover SUV produced by Chinese manufacturer Li Auto. It is the fourth vehicle from the manufacturer, and also the third L-series model, a series of electric SUVs with a petrol engine acting as a range extender, which includes the similar L9, L8 and L6.
