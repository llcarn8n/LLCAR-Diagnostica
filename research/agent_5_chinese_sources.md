## Executive Summary

China is Li Auto's primary and dominant market with over 1 million L-series vehicles delivered (as of April 2025). Chinese complaint platforms, forums, and media reveal a pattern of recurring quality issues — most critically a **systemic lower control arm (下摆臂) noise problem** affecting ALL L-series models in 2025, phantom braking from ADAS misidentification, and structural safety concerns (AB-pillar fracture, brake failures). The brand's complaint-to-sales ratio deteriorated significantly from 2023 (best in class) to 2025 (among worst NEV brands). Li Auto's response pattern — denying issues, blaming owners, then issuing delayed fixes — is a recurring theme in Chinese media.

## Critical Issues Found

### 1. Lower Control Arm (下摆臂) Noise — Systemic Defect Across ALL L-Series (2025)
- **Models**: L6, L7, L8, L9 (all 2025 "Smart Refresh" / 智能焕新版)
- **Mileage**: 1,500–5,000 km (appears shortly after delivery)
- **Symptoms**: Steering column "creaking" (嘎吱声), chassis rattling, left/right lower arm noise during turning and going over bumps. Noise persists or recurs even after part replacement.
- **Affected Systems**: Front suspension — lower control arm bushings (hydraulic bushings with new grease formulation)
- **Root Cause**: Li Auto officially acknowledged in July 2025 that the 2025 models use a new hydraulic bushing grease that changes viscosity at high temperatures, causing intermittent noise.
- **Resolution**: Li Auto offered 3-year/60,000 km front suspension extended warranty, extended front suspension warranty to 8 years/160,000 km. Replacement of lower control arms available. However, owners report inconsistent compensation across dealerships — some get 2,000 RMB "care fee", others get nothing.
- **Scale**: ~400 owners self-organized in a WeChat rights-protection group. 282 complaints filed on 12365auto.com in July 2025 alone (as of July 24). Li Auto L7 complaint index surged 1,383.48% month-over-month.
- **Historical Pattern**: This is NOT new — Li Auto ONE was recalled in November 2020 for the same component (lower control arm ball joint detachment), affecting 10,469 vehicles. The recurring nature suggests a fundamental design weakness in the front suspension across generations.
- **Confidence**: HIGH
- **Sources**: [Sina News](https://news.sina.com.cn/minsheng/2025-09-21/doc-infrhiah2415858.shtml), [Sohu Auto](https://www.sohu.com/a/918183725_362225), [财中社](https://m.caizhongshe.cn/article-6630992144641519464.html), [OFweek](https://mp.ofweek.com/nev/a056714896507), [QQ News](https://news.qq.com/rain/a/20250726A070MO00), [车质网 L7](https://www.12365auto.com/series/c-3432-1-1.shtml)
- **How Found**: "理想汽车 下摆臂 异响 维权 2025", "理想L7 质量问题 投诉 2024 2025"

### 2. ADAS Phantom Braking / Misidentification — Billboard & Ghost Detection
- **Models**: L7, L9
- **Mileage**: Various
- **Symptoms**: Automatic Emergency Braking (AEB) system triggers at highway speeds (80–120 km/h) after misidentifying images on billboards, advertisements, or non-existent objects as real obstacles. Vehicle brakes hard from highway speed to near-zero.
- **Affected Systems**: ADAS (camera + radar fusion), AEB
- **Notable Incidents**:
  - L9 mistook a truck image on a highway billboard advertisement for a real vehicle, triggered AEB, and was rear-ended by following traffic
  - L7 identified sprinter Su Bingtian's starting pose on a roadside billboard as a pedestrian, braking from 80 km/h to 20 km/h
  - L9 camera detected "humans and cyclists" near a cemetery with nobody present (2023)
- **Root Cause**: Software algorithm insufficient to distinguish 2D images from real 3D objects
- **Resolution**: Li Auto stated it would improve software systems via OTA. 2025 models have upgraded to lidar + camera fusion.
- **Scale**: Multiple documented incidents, exact complaint count unknown
- **Confidence**: HIGH
- **Sources**: [The Autopian](https://www.theautopian.com/suv-with-emergency-braking-mistakes-billboard-ad-for-real-car-and-slams-on-brakes/), [中新网](http://www.chinanews.com.cn/cj/2024/05-12/10215638.shtml), [QQ News](https://news.qq.com/rain/a/20240809A07OLX00)
- **How Found**: "理想L9 高速自动急刹", "Li Auto L9 phantom brake ADAS"

### 3. Air Suspension Failure — L9 Historical & Ongoing
- **Models**: L9 (primarily), L7/L8 (with air suspension option)
- **Mileage**: As early as pre-delivery (test drive incident, 2022); 10,000–15,000 km for compressor issues
- **Season**: Winter exacerbates compressor problems
- **Symptoms**:
  - 2022: Test drive L9 wheel collapsed into body at 90 km/h after hitting 20 cm pothole — air spring buffer ring failed
  - Ongoing: Air suspension compressor squeaks and failures, especially in winter; moisture/reagent infiltration
  - Air pump noise (气泵异响) reported by L7 Ultra owner within 2 months of purchase
- **Affected Systems**: Air suspension — springs (buffer rings), compressor, control module
- **Suppliers**: Vibracoustic (威巴克), Baolong Technology (保隆科技), Konghui Technology (孔辉科技)
- **Root Cause**: 2022 incident: non-production buffer ring 2.5x weaker than production part. Ongoing: compressor vulnerability to moisture, reagent degradation
- **Resolution**: Li Auto extended air suspension warranty to 5 years after 2022 incident. 2025 models upgraded to dual-chamber dual-circuit air suspension.
- **Confidence**: HIGH (2022 incident), MEDIUM (ongoing compressor issues — more prevalent in Russian market)
- **Sources**: [澎湃新闻](https://www.thepaper.cn/newsDetail_forward_19075788), [中新网](https://www.chinanews.com.cn/cj/2022/07-18/9806437.shtml), [观察者网](https://www.guancha.cn/economy/2022_07_18_649873.shtml), [CnEVPost](https://cnevpost.com/2022/07/18/li-auto-in-spotlight-for-li-l9-test-car-suspension-breakage/)
- **How Found**: "理想L9 悬架 异响 空气悬架 故障", "Li Auto L9 air suspension compressor failure"

### 4. Rear Reducer Bolt Fracture — Highway Sudden Stop (L9)
- **Models**: L9 (2024 model, top trim)
- **Mileage**: 2,575 km
- **Symptoms**: Vehicle suddenly vibrated and stalled on highway (长浏高速), complete power loss, unable to restart. Rear reducer bolt fractured, all reducer oil leaked.
- **Affected Systems**: Rear drivetrain — rear reducer/differential
- **Root Cause**: Li Auto claims external impact (road debris) broke the bolt. Owner disputes this — asks why no warning sensors exist on such a critical component, and why a small stone can disable the entire vehicle.
- **Resolution**: Li Auto quoted ~40,000 RMB repair cost; no recall or warranty extension offered. Owner demanded vehicle return.
- **Historical Pattern**: Li Auto ONE also had multiple rear reducer failures reported previously.
- **Scale**: Individual documented case, but pattern with Li Auto ONE suggests recurring design vulnerability
- **Confidence**: MEDIUM (individual case, but recurring pattern)
- **Sources**: [红车网](https://auto.rednet.cn/content/646842/99/14165398.html), [长沙晚报](https://www.icswb.com/h/156/20240806/883564.html), [汽车之家](https://chejiahao.m.autohome.com.cn/info/16229636)

### 7. Infotainment System — Black Screen, Freezing, OTA Issues

### 8. Range Extender — Fuel Consumption & Winter Range Degradation
- **Models**: L7, L9
- **Season**: Winter (critical), year-round for fuel economy
- **Symptoms**:
  - Depleted battery (亏电) fuel consumption: 8.1–12.05 L/100km depending on conditions (highway 120 km/h: ~9.5 L/100km)
  - Pure electric range: L9 official CLTC 215 km, real-world test ~144 km
  - Winter range drops to 150–180 km vs 280 km standard (46–50% reduction)
  - Range extender noise noticeable at high RPM
- **Root Cause**: Physical battery chemistry limitations in cold weather; range extender operates at high RPM for adequate power generation
- **Resolution**: Li Auto offers optional winter kit claiming ≤15% range loss at -30°C. 2025 models have larger batteries.
- **Confidence**: HIGH (well-documented, inherent to EREV technology)
- **Sources**: [Autohome L9 forum](https://club.m.autohome.com.cn/bbs/thread/a6f7213ecf3cf9be/110438906-1.html), [Bilibili](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1Kk4y1q7XQ/), [知乎](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/487642222), [新出行](https://www.xchuxing.com/article/64861), [42号车库](https://www.42how.com/en/article/7382)

## Official Recalls in China

### Li Auto ONE Recall — November 2020
- **Scope**: 10,469 vehicles produced before June 1, 2020
- **Issue**: Front suspension lower control arm ball joint detachment risk
- **Fix**: Free replacement of lower control arms with upgraded version
- **Regulator**: SAMR (国家市场监督管理总局)
- **Notable**: Li Auto initially called it an "upgrade" rather than a "recall", was publicly criticized, then apologized
- **Sources**: [Li Auto IR](https://ir.lixiang.com/news-releases/news-release-details/li-auto-inc-announces-voluntary-recall), [SCMP](https://www.scmp.com/business/companies/article/3108852/nasdaq-listed-chinese-electric-carmaker-li-auto-apologies)

### Li Auto MEGA Recall — November 2025
- **Scope**: 11,411 units of MEGA 2024, produced Feb 18 – Dec 27, 2024
- **Issue**: Coolant anti-corrosion performance insufficient → cooling plate corrosion/leakage in battery and front motor controller → potential thermal runaway
- **Trigger**: Shanghai fire incident on October 23, 2025
- **Fix**: Free replacement of power battery and related components
- **Financial Impact**: ~11 billion RMB ($1.55 billion) liability booked
- **Regulator**: SAMR, recall number S2025M0174V
- **Note**: Li Auto confirmed this does NOT affect L7/L8/L9 models
- **Sources**: [CarNewsChina](https://carnewschina.com/2025/10/31/li-auto-recalls-over-11000-mega-2024-models-due-to-potential-battery-thermal-runaway-risk/), [SAMR DPAC](https://www.samrdprc.org.cn/qczh/qczhgg1/202511/t20251112_114526.html), [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-10-31/li-auto-recalls-over-11-000-of-its-flagship-ev-on-battery-fire)

### No L7/L8/L9 Recalls Found
Despite numerous quality complaints, no official SAMR/DPAC recalls have been issued for L7, L8, or L9 models as of early 2026. The 2025 lower control arm issue was handled through warranty extension rather than formal recall.

## Chinese vs International Issues — Key Differences

| Issue | China | Russia/Export |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| **Lower control arm noise** | Massive 2025 complaints, warranty extension | Less documented |
| **Air suspension compressor** | Fewer complaints (better roads) | Major issue (bad roads, winter moisture) |
| **Winter range degradation** | Acknowledged, winter kit available | Critical problem (-50% range) |
| **Price cut protests** | Huge social media backlash, organized protests | Less relevant (parallel import pricing different) |
| **ADAS phantom braking** | Well-documented billboard incidents | Not widely reported |
| **Service/warranty** | Official dealer network, extended warranties | Until 2025: no warranty (parallel imports); now establishing official dealers |
| **OTA updates** | Regular updates, some controversy | Same software, but no official support until 2025 |
| **Road condition damage** | Less frequent (highways mostly good) | Rear reducer bolt fracture, suspension damage from poor roads |

**Key insight**: In China, the most frequent complaints are about quality control and build consistency (lower control arm, interior noise, electronics). In Russia, complaints center on durability under harsh conditions (cold, bad roads, lack of service network). The underlying platform weaknesses (suspension design, brake system) manifest differently depending on operating environment.
